# The Future of FinTech: Tokenization

**THE CURATOR | The Architectural Reconfiguration of Global Capital Markets**

A long-form editorial piece exploring how tokenization is transforming the way we think about ownership, settlement, and access to real-world assets.

---

## What This Is

This is a self-contained, interactive web article that breaks down the concept of asset tokenization from first principles. It covers what tokenization is, how it works, why it matters, and what it means for the future of financial markets.

Written and designed as part of The Curator series, a collection of research-driven editorial pieces on emerging technology and finance.

---

## What It Covers

- A plain-English definition of tokenization and how blockchain ledgers enable it
- The process of converting real-world assets into programmable, tradeable digital tokens
- The impact on asset classes including real estate, private equity, and commodities
- Why institutions are moving toward tokenized infrastructure
- The challenges and risks that still stand in the way
