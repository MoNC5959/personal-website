BOEING INSPECTOR — HOW TO USE
YOUR ROLE
ASSEMBLY LINE QUALITY INSPECTOR
You are inspecting a 737 wing assembly section against its digital twin. The AI scans each component zone in real time and flags any dimensional variances or structural defects before the part advances down the line.
WHAT TO DO
01 Click BEGIN SCAN in the sidebar to start the inspection sequence. The AI will sweep each of the 5 checkpoint zones in order.
02 Watch the cyan scan beam sweep across the wing diagram. Live readings appear in the HUD overlay at the bottom left.
03 Click the pulsing red or orange markers that appear on the diagram to open the defect detail panel — showing measured vs. spec values, tolerance track, and required action.
04 Toggle SPEC VIEW in the top bar to overlay the digital twin zone boundaries on the wing diagram at any time.
05 After all 5 steps complete, click VIEW AUDIT TRAIL & SIGN OFF to review every checkpoint result and lock the inspection record.
TIP: Each scan step advances automatically. The checklist on the right tracks your progress. Steps with defects will show a red FAIL badge — the assembly hold is triggered automatically at the end.
